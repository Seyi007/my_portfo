01 : About me

My Background

Hi there! I’m Seyi Kuforiji – and I’m obsessed with all things Web Development. I’m driven by technology and programming. I love impactful work, especially when it involves creating services others benefit from. As a Back-End engineer, I build and manage the server-side of a website by using LAMP stack and Django framework. Professionally, I’m motivated by the different facets of technology and its endless possibilities. Outside of work, I recharge by watching football, movies, and playing video games. What I appreciate about this field is being able to pursue my personal goals and professional passions with equal enthusiasm – honestly, I think it makes me a better person. I’m always happy to connect with like-minded web developers. If you’d like to contact me about anything web dev, visit my contact page to reach out!.

Educational Background

In 2023, I received a certification in Software Engineering with a specialization in Back-End development from ALX Africa and Holberton School. In 2021, I obtained a BSc degree in Geography And Planning from the university of Lagos, Nigeria.

Work Experience

In my career so far I have: 1. Created a weather forecast website that predicts the atmospheric conditions of any given location using Python programing language, OpenWeather API, Django web framework, and SQLite database, to show real-time weather data, such as; rainfall, humidity, wind direction, atmospheric temperature, and much more. 2. Built a local library website that provides a simple online catalog for a small local library, where users log in and get information about the books the library has in its inventory and their availability. It also provides users the ability to make a request to borrow from available books. The website was built using Python programing language, Django web framework, and SQLite database. Outside the tech ecosystem, I worked as an intern at Bi-Courtney Aviation Services, a company operating and managing the domestic airport in Lagos, Nigeria, in 2019. In 2020, I worked as a Supervisor at a gas station, where I gained some valuable leadership and management skills.
